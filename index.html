ABOUT JINJUU

Jinjuu, meaning 'pearl', is founded by celebrity chef Judy Joo.
Located in the iconic, newly built California Tower, Jinjuu seamlessly merges traditional and contemporary styles of cuisine and design, aims to give the guests an unique Korean dining experience.
